About Me

Cristiano Ronaldo (CR7) G.O.A.T

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Raised in a modest background. Ronaldo’s passion for football was evident from a young age. His father’s struggle with alcoholism and his mother’s efforts as a cook and cleaner shaped his early years. Despite hardships, his love for the game fueled his determination to excel.
